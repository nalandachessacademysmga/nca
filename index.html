Nalanda Chess Academy is dedicated to fostering a love for chess and nurturing talent from beginners to advanced players. We provide a supportive environment for players to develop their skills, engage in friendly competition, and connect with a passionate community.

Our commitment is to make chess accessible and enjoyable for everyone, promoting strategic thinking and personal growth through this timeless game.
